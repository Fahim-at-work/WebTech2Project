A Message from President Judith Morris

We have always had a reputation as a groundbreaking College. Our work in applied research, technology, health, international programming, social projects, and new sectors such as the bio industry and esports, continue to exceed all expectations. As a small college, our achievements are large, and reflective of our career-focused programs, exceptional faculty and staff, and brand new, world-class facilities.

Not only are we opening new buildings, such as our new NOVA Chemicals Health & Research Centre, and Athletics & Fitness Complex, we are also in the process of developing our new Strategic Plan, which will guide us into 2024. Since that very first day, back in November 1967, Lambton College has worked in partnership with our community to create a region of innovators, working together to strengthen our local economy, and meet industry demand for skilled employees.

It’s this collaborative process that led to initiatives such as our newly opened Centre of Excellence in Energy & Bio-Industrial Technologies. Built on a foundation of distinctive programs, applied research, evolving teaching methodologies, and experiential learning opportunities, this new Centre allows us to ensure our region will meet the skills and training required for the future of the emerging bio-based industry. Advancements in technology have also played a role in transforming the learning landscape at our College. As new technologies emerge and alter the way we live and work, it has also helped us formulate the education and training required to prepare our graduates to navigate the impending 4th Industrial Revolution.

Aesthetically, you’ll also notice Lambton College has a new look and feel. Our new brand and accompanying shield, unveiled in May 2018, visually represent our values, while celebrating our bright future and our daily commitment to ensuring the success of our students. While nobody can predict exactly what the future holds, as a College, we will continue to harness our existing expertise, embracing change and adapting to emerging technologies, so we can ensure our graduates are trained to compete in a rapidly changing global job market.

  • Judith Morris
  • President & CEO
  • Lambton College
