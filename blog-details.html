Event Centre

The Lambton College Event Centre can offer you space for any function or event. The Event Centre can accommodate functions of 10 - 400 attendees in banquet, theatre, classroom or breakout session style setups.
Academic Upgrading is an in-class, free, flexible, supportive program designed to help students meet their individual college, apprenticeship or employment goals. Our in-class program guides students back into the academic world by creating a plan to bring their reading, writing and other essential skills up to the end of high school level.
