Our Board

Lambton College is a community college whose staff, faculty, and administrators provide quality post-secondary education in Southwestern Ontario. Nationally renowned for its ability to align programs with the needs of industry, Lambton College currently boasts the highest employer satisfaction rate in Ontario, according to the latest Key Performance Indicator (KPI) report and ranks first in Canada for Applied Research. These rankings reinforce what the College has worked hard to achieve: a supportive and innovative campus environment that bridges the gap between education and industry.

With the recent upgrade and renovation of the Centre of Excellence in Energy & Bio-Industrial Technology, the Athletics & Fitness Complex and the NOVA Chemicals Health & Research Centre, there's never been a better time to be part of the Lambton College team. The college is governed by a volunteer Board of Governors that is responsible for setting policy, establishing strategic directions, and monitoring organizational performance. The nominating committee of the Board of Governors recommends for approval by the board, individuals committed to actively fulfilling the role of governor for a three-year term.

Lambton College is currently recruiting for two new Governors to begin September 1, 2020 with experience in the environmental/bio-industrial/research or health sectors. If you are interested in applying for this position, please submit a completed application form by Tuesday, December 10, 2019 to lianne.birkbeck@lambtoncollege.ca.
